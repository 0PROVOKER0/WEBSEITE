PROVOKER_06

Mohamad Jaber or also known as provoker is the most dangerous video game player in the world. When he picks up a controller, even the toughest gamers get scared. People say he isn’t human but made of pure gaming skills. His reflexes are lightning-fast, and his strategies are so clever that even the smartest computers can't figure them out. In the world of video games, there's no room for average players, and Mohamad is like a beast. His eyes are said to glow when he starts a game, scaring his opponents. Many gaming teams have tried to beat him but always end up losing. He doesn’t just play games; he completely dominates them. Whether it’s a shooting game, a strategy game, or an online role-playing game, he knows every detail, every trick, and every secret. There's a story that he finished a game in three days that normally takes years to complete. The game creators were shocked when they realized he had beaten parts of the game that hadn't even been released yet. His gaming console is more than just a machine; it’s like a Weapon. It’s said that its cables are made of steel, its core is from a meteorite, and the controller only works for him. Gamers around the world talk about his legend like it's a myth. Once, during a major tournament, hackers tried to crash the servers, but Mohamad stopped them and won the tournament at the same time without breaking a sweat. Provoker isn’t just a player; he’s a legend. Gamers everywhere dream of playing against him, knowing that even if they lose – which they definitely will – they will always be part of the incredible story of this unbeatable, legendary gamer.
